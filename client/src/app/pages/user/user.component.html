Detection from X-Rays

Dr. Lungs

Artificial Radiologist powered by Azure Vision

A team of researchers from Qatar University, Doha, Qatar and the University of Dhaka, Bangladesh along with their collaborators from Pakistan and Malaysia in collaboration with medical doctors have created a database of chest X-ray images for COVID-19 positive cases along with Normal and Viral Pneumonia images. In this component of the application we employ that dataset to detect COVID from Chest X-rays.
